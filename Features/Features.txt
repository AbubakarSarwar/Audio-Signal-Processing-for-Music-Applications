Features:

1. Chromagram(also known as pitch class profile)- closely relates to 12 different pitch classes. Powerful tool for analyzing music whose pitches can be meaningfully categorized(often into 12 categories) and whose tuning approximates to the equal tempered scale. Main property of the chroma feature is that they capture harmonic and melodic characteristics of music, while being robust to changes in timbre and instrumentation. 
Pitch Class Set: {C, C♯, D, D♯, E ,F, F♯, G, G♯, A, A♯, B}
